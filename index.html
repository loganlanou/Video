How to Embed a Video into Your Website

Step 1: Find the Hosting Service

Go to the video hosting service Example: YouTube.

Step 2: Locate The Video

Find the desired video and click on it.

Step 3: Copy the URL

Find the URL in the search bar and copy it

Step 4: Paste the URL Into Your HTML

Paste the video URL in your code after properly formatting if to be embedded.

Step 5: Commit Your Code to Github and Reload Your Website

Travel to your commit tab and title the commit. Then reload your website.
